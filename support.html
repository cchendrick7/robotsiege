Robot Siege — Support

Thanks for playing Robot Siege! We're happy to help.

Contact

For questions, bug reports, or help with a purchase, email robotsiegesupport@MeritDigitalGroup.com. Please include your device model and iOS version so we can help faster.

Restoring purchases

Bought the Commander or 2× Speed Boost and don't see it? Open the in-game Shop and tap Restore Purchases (top right). Token packs and the Token Boost are consumable and are not restorable, per Apple's rules.

Frequently asked

Do I need internet? No — Robot Siege plays fully offline.

Where is my progress saved? On your device. Deleting the app erases it.
