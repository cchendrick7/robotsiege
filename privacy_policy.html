Robot Siege — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Robot Siege ("the app", "we", "us") handles information. By using the app you agree to this policy.

The short version

Robot Siege does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising. Your game progress is saved only on your own device.

Information we collect

None. We do not collect names, email addresses, location, contacts, device identifiers, or any other personal data. The app does not require you to create an account or sign in.

Game data & storage

Your progress (levels, tokens, upgrades, settings) is stored locally on your device by the app. It is not transmitted to us or to any server. Deleting the app removes this data from your device.

In-app purchases

Robot Siege offers optional in-app purchases. All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details. Apple's handling of your purchase information is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Third-party services

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. The only third party involved is Apple, solely to process in-app purchases as described above.

Children's privacy

Robot Siege is suitable for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because the app collects no personal data at all, no such information about children is gathered or stored.

Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices change (for example, if crash reporting or Game Center features are added in a future update), we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date above.
